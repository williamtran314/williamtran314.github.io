Sound Society

Sound Society at UC Santa Barbara is a registered campus organization dedicated to providing a community for musicians and music producers of all skill levels at UCSB. We aim to promote accessibility to music production for UCSB students, focusing on education, mentorship, and collaboration.

As the founder and former president of Sound Society, I managed our 175+ member Discord server, planned and executed weekly general meetings, and oversaw a staff of 15 student leaders.

I also produced a large amount of visual content for the organization, from graphic design work to live concert performance videos.
