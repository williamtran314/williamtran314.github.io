Sound Society

Sound Society at UC Santa Barbara is a registered campus organization dedicated to providing a community for musicians and music producers of all skill levels at UCSB. We aim to promote accessibility to music production for UCSB students, focusing on education, mentorship, and collaboration.

As the founder and president of Sound Society, I manage our 175+ member Discord server, plan and execute weekly general meetings, and oversee a staff of 15 student leaders.

I also produce a large amount of visual content for the organization, from graphic design work to live concert performance videos.
