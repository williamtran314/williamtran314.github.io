Dungeons & Donors (Apra Data Science Now 2024 Finalist)

In this project, my team and I built a 5-year campaign for a simulated higher-education organization based around forecasts from time-series modeling. This was done as a submission to Apra's 2024 Data Science Challenge, a yearly competition hosted by Apra for analytics professionals working in the non-profit and higher education sectors. Our project was selected as a featured presentation at Apra's national 2024 Prospect Development Conference.

Working with my team at Saint Louis University, I handled data cleaning, conducted exploratory data analysis, contributed to the time series model, and created/verified the donor pyramid. I also handled creative direction, establishing the visual identity of the project with graphics and logos created using Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop.

This project was done using a combination of Python, R, Tableau, and Excel.
